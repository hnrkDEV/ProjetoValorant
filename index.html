VYSE

ARC ROSE is primarily a Blind ability that Vyse must equip before casting. Once equipped, her crosshair will be replaced with an indicator for placing the rose. This will turn blue if it is placed on a viable position to be cast in, and will turn red for unviable positions. Arc Rose can also be cast on the opposite side of viable surface by using Alternate Fire. Upon casting, the rose will travel to the location and briefly deploy, then it will turn invisible and remain at its position indefinitely.

RAZORVINE is a Crowd Control ability that Vyse must equip before casting. Upon casting, she will launch a nest until it lands on a horizontal surface, after which it will turn invisible and remain indefinitely. During Buy Phase, the nest can be picked up to regain the charge, but once the round has started it cannot be recalled.

SHEAR is primarily an Intel ability that Vyse must equip before casting. Once equipped, her crosshair will be replaced with an indicator for placing the trap. This will turn purple if it is placed on a viable position to be cast in, and a red marker for unviable positions. A viable position must be placed on a wall perpendicular to the ground. Upon casting, the trap will travel to the location and briefly deploy. During Buy Phase, the trap can be picked up to regain the charge, but once the round has started it cannot be recalled.

STEEL GARDEN is a Limiter ability that Vyse must equip before casting. Upon casting, she will deploy a torrent of metal thorns in a large area surrounding her. Thorns will appear on any terrain at any altitude within the area, and the area of effect has no maximum height limit within the playable space of the map.
